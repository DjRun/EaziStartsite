About

The Startup and Innovation Center was founded by the management of the Azerbaijan State Oil & Industry University in November 2015. The first trainings featuring foreign mentors were launched in January 2016. Foreign business incubators and mentors took an active role in establishing the Center and designing its activities. Each idea is assessed through several phases with the involvement of business experts and specialists of the related field. Once the idea is approved by the Startup and Innovation Center, both the Center and ASOIU management seeks for fundsand investments for realizing it..

Eazi START Startup and Innovation Center aims to convert the ideas of our students into startup projects and commercialize them in global markets. This Center is designed to realize our main mission: to help our students graduate from university not only with diploma, but also with their own companies

Wish you all an eazi start with us!
